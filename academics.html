Academics

A structured, engaging curriculum from Nursery through Class 10.

Curriculum

Grades & Streams We Offer

Our curriculum follows the state board syllabus, enriched with activity-based and value-based learning.

📜

Pre-Primary

Nursery, LKG & UKG — play-based learning focused on early motor, language, and social skills.

🏛️

Primary (I – V)

Building strong foundations in language, mathematics, and environmental studies through interactive teaching.

🦚

Upper Primary (VI – VIII)

Broader subject exposure with an emphasis on conceptual understanding and analytical thinking.

🐘

Secondary (IX – X)

Rigorous, exam-focused preparation across Sciences, Mathematics, Social Studies, and Languages.

🏺

Languages

Telugu, English, and Hindi taught to build strong reading, writing, and communication skills.

🏹

Science & Math Labs

Regular practical sessions to reinforce classroom learning through hands-on experiments.
